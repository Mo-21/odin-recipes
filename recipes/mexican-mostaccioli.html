Mexican Mostaccioli

Budget-friendly, Mexican-inspired Pasta dish

Description

Ground beef, stewed tomatoes, pasta, and picante sauce combine with Mexican-style cheese for a comforting, budget dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package mostaccioli pasta
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 (24 ounce) jar picante sauce
  • 1 (15 ounce) can stewed tomatoes with juice
  • 1 Â½ cups shredded Mexican-style cheese

Directions

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook mostaccioli at a boil until cooked through yet firm to the bite, about 11 minutes; drain.
  2. Cook and stir ground beef in a large pot over medium-high heat until crumbly, evenly browned, and no longer pink, 5 to 7 minutes.
  3. Add picante sauce and stewed tomatoes to the ground beef; cook, stirring occasionally, until the tomatoes soften completely, about 10 minutes.
  4. Gently stir the cooked mostaccioli into the ground beef mixture. Add Mexican-style cheese; cook and stir until the cheese is melted, about 5 minutes.
  5. Enjoy your meal!
